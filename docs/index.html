What is the Tech Radar?

The EPAM OSTD Tech Radar is a list of technologies, identified by the Practice based on the technologie used in many project and upcoming projects from teh customers.

  • ADOPT — This is a clear recommendation for our Associate to adopt to the technology as we consider it to be a major contribution to success in the future.
  • TRIAL — We assume the technology to be very useful for most. It should be evaluated in a trial project to create awareness.
  • ASSESS — The technology has potential, and EPAM should evaluate its value. The company should also check the usage of these by their clients and conduct POC be ready for adoption.
  • HOLD — These technologies are not being accessed as there is less demand from our customer, but will be picked up if demand changes.

What is the purpose?

The Tech Radar is a tool to inspire and support Engineering teams at EPAM to pick the best technologies; it provides a platform to share knowledge and experience in technologies, to reflect on technology decisions and continuously evolve our technology landscape. Based on the pioneering work of ThoughtWorks, our Tech Radar sets out the changes in technologies that are interesting in software development — changes that we think our engineering teams should pay attention to and use in their projects.

How do we maintain it?

The Tech Radar is maintained by our OSTD Practice — who will faciliate and drive the technology, and help in creating learning plan and incubation across the Community. The Tech radar wil lbe reviewed every quater, and will be publised on every update, the changes may also be based on customer and industry adoption.
